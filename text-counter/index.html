Real-time text counter

Paste or type text and monitor counts instantly while editing.

A text counter is essential when writing for platforms with strict limits, such as social posts, ad copy, metadata fields, titles, and app descriptions. Instead of counting manually or guessing, this page reports totals as you type. You can monitor full character count, no-space count, word count, line count, and UTF-8 byte size at the same time. That makes it useful for editors, marketers, developers, and anyone preparing content that must fit exactly.

The counter runs in your browser and does not upload text to a server. This is especially important for private drafts, internal notes, and pre-release copy. Because the summary updates in real time, you can trim content while watching limits directly. After counting, copy the summary for reports or download it as a local file. If you need to clean, reformat, or normalize your text before final submission, related links below connect to cleanup, case conversion, and list-structure tools.

FAQ

Does this count spaces?

Yes. You get both total characters and no-space characters.

How are words counted?

Words are split by whitespace, so repeated spaces are ignored.

Can I check byte size too?

Yes. UTF-8 byte count is included in real time.

Does this work for multiline text?

Yes. Line count updates for pasted paragraphs and list data.

Is my text stored anywhere?

No. Input stays in your browser and is not uploaded.

Can I export the summary?

Yes. Copy Summary or Download .txt both work.
